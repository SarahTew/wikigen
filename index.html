How do Wikidata descriptions of male and female art historians compare?

Granularity

How detailed is the portrait Wikidata paints?

  • Wikidata describes the average male art historian in 102 properties but the average female art historian in 72.
  • Female art historians are described with 30% fewer properties than male art historians.

Authority Linking

How does Wikidata link art historians to VIAF?

How does Wikidata link art historians to other external authorities?

Personal Relationships

How does Wikidata describe art historians' family relationships?

  • 10% of female art historians have at least one personal relationship declared.
  • 13% of male art historians have at least one personal relationship declared.

Personal Relationships

What does Wikidata say about art historians with declared personal relationships?

  • Overall, male art historian's personal relationships are more likely to be declared than female art historian's.
  • The most common personal relationship property for male art historians was father.
  • The most common personal relationship property for female art historians was spouse.
  • 59% of female art historians have a spouse declared while only 34% of male art historians do.

Professional Relationships

How does Wikidata describe art historians' professional relationships?

  • 13% of male art historians have at least one professional relationship declared.
  • 10 percent of female art historians have at least one professional relationship declared.

Professional Relationships

What does Wikidata say about art historians' professional relationships?

  • Male art historian's professional relationships are more likely to be declared than female art historian's.
  • Doctoral advisors and teachers (property: student of) are more likely to be declared than students.
  • The most common professional relationship property for male art historians was student of.
  • The most common professional relationship property for female art historians was doctoral advisor.
  • 49% of female art historians have a doctoral advisor declared while only 20% of male art historians do.
